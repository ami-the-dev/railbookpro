# BookingFlow Design System

## Overview

BookingFlow is an efficient, calendar-centric design system for hotel and flight booking engines. Trust and clarity are paramount — every element designed to reduce booking anxiety and guide users through complex selections.

## Colors

- **Primary** (#1D4ED8): Primary actions, navigation
- **Secondary** (#059669): Deals, best price, savings
- **Tertiary** (#D97706): Alerts, price changes, urgency
- **Background** (#FFFFFF): Page background
- **Surface** (#FFFFFF): Cards, modals
- **Success** (#059669)
- **Warning** (#D97706)
- **Error** (#EF4444)
- **Info** (#2563EB)

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do display prices in monospace with currency symbol for instant scannability
- Don't hide total pricing behind expandable sections
- Don't require more than three steps to complete a booking
